How to refer

As an Affiliate Referrer, you can reach more potential borrowers by promoting us online. Register your social media or email accounts, and we’ll give you:

  • A unique Affiliate Link for posts or emails
  • A unique Affiliate Code to associate your referrals to you.

Note: To protect your privacy and safety, avoid using SMS for promotions.

Earn from referrals

Referring is simple: share the basic details of a business owner who may need short-term financing via our referral form. We will call him to process his application, and keep you notified of developments. If his application converts to a loan released, you will earn the following.

  • 1% of the loan principal when a loan is released
  • 1% on successful referral loans of your direct client, up to the 3rd level
  • 1% on successful renewal loans by your direct clients

Ready to start earning?

  1. Fill out the Affiliate Form on the right to sign up.
  2. After sign-up, we will send your unique Affiliate Code and Affiliate Link by email.
  3. Attach your Affiliate Link to every promotion you make.
  4. Fill out the Referral Form or call us to start referring customers.
  5. Stay Informed. We’ll update you by email and phone.
  6. Claim your commission. Rewards are credited directly to your bank account, hassle-free.
