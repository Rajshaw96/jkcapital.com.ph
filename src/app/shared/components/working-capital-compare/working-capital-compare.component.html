How It Differs from a Standard Business Loan

Working Capital Loan Business Loan
Purpose Covers short-term operational needs like payroll, rent, and inventory. Supports long-term growth, expansion, or asset purchases.
Loan Term Short-term (weeks to months). Short-term (weeks to months).
Approval Time Fast, often 5-7 days. Longer, depending on size and documentation.
Disbursement Funded as a one-time loan. Fixed lump sum disbursed upfront.
Collateral Often secured by receivables, POs, or post-dated checks. May require fixed assets or personal guarantees.
Best For Managing cash flow gaps and daily operational expenses. Funding growth, expansion, or major capital investments.
