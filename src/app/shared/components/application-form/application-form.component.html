We’re recruiting: Loan Consultants

We’re looking for detail-oriented, results-driven individuals who will to help SMEs find the right financing solutions. You’ll guide customers through the loan process—from inquiry to approval—ensuring a smooth and positive experience.

Responsibilities:

  • Master JK Capital products through JK Capital trainings and consultations.
  • Explain loan products and assist clients through applications.
  • Build and maintain strong client relationships.
  • Stay updated on loan offerings and credit guidelines from JK Capital.
  • Support renewals, refinancing, and underwriting coordination.
  • Compliance with company policies and regulations.

Qualifications:

  • At least 25 years old, male or female.
  • Experience in loans, finance, or sales (insurance, real estate, pharma, etc.) is an advantage.
  • Knowledge of loan products and credit evaluation preferred.
  • Excellent communication and customer service skills.
  • Undergraduates with relevant experience are welcome. College degree is a bonus.

Benefits:

  • 3% full agent commission per successful loan — fast payout upon release.
  • Up to 2% commission on direct client renewal loans.
  • 1% commission on successful referral loans by direct clients, up to 3rd level.
  • No quotas, no office hours—earn based on performance and results.

Get started:

  • Fill out and submit the Loan Consultant Application Form on the right. And we'll inform you on next steps to get accredited.
  • Once accredited, you are good to go.
  • Call JK Capital-Sales to facilitate customer loan applications.
