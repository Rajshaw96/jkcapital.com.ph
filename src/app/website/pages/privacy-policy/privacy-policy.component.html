Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

We comply with Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012), its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Republic Act No. 1405 (Law on Secrecy of Bank Deposits), and all other applicable Philippine data privacy laws, as well as regulations of the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas and other relevant authorities. Protecting your privacy is both our legal duty and our commitment to you as our client.

Information We Collect

We collect personal and non-personal data when you visit our website, apply for products or services, submit inquiries or complaints, or interact with us in person or by phone.

Identification & Contact Data

  • Name, address, email, mobile number, birthdate
  • Educational background and specimen signatures
  • Government-issued IDs, TIN, SSS/GSIS, driver’s license

Financial & Employment Details

  • Income, expenses, deposits, investments
  • Credit card information
  • Employer, position, office visit records
  • CCTV footage and access logs

Voice, Transaction & Usage Data

  • Recorded calls (with consent)
  • Payment history, utilities, third-party services
  • IP address, browser type, OS, cookies, analytics

Why We Collect Your Data

  • To process and service loan applications
  • To assess creditworthiness and manage accounts
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • To conduct analytics, research, and profiling
  • To communicate updates, offers, and customer support

How We Obtain Consent

  • Explicit, informed consent before collecting sensitive data
  • Consent may be withdrawn at any time via our Data Protection Officer

How We Use Your Data

  • Deliver requested products and services
  • Prevent fraud and enhance security
  • Generate internal and regulatory reports
  • Personalize offers and improve marketing performance

Who We Share Your Data With

Affiliates & Service Providers

  • IT support providers
  • Payment processors
  • Marketing and analytics partners

Regulatory & Law Enforcement

  • Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP)
  • National Privacy Commission (NPC)
  • SEC, AMLC, courts (as required by law)

Credit Reference Agencies

  • Credit Information Corporation
  • Private credit bureaus

Contractual Partners

  • Appraisers, insurers, debt collectors (with consent)

Cross-Border Transfers

Your data may be transferred to countries with adequate data protection safeguards. We apply Standard Contractual Clauses, Binding Corporate Rules, or obtain your explicit consent where required.

Cookies & Web Analytics

We use cookies and third-party analytics tools to collect anonymous usage data. You may manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

How We Protect Your Data

  • Technical safeguards: encryption, firewalls, multi-factor authentication
  • Organizational measures: appointed DPO, staff training
  • Physical security: secured offices, CCTV, locked storage

Data Retention & Disposal

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for its purpose and legal obligations. Secure disposal includes document shredding and cryptographic erasure.

Your Rights

  • Right to be informed and access your data
  • Right to rectification, erasure, or blocking
  • Right to object to processing and data portability
  • Right to damages and file complaints with the NPC
