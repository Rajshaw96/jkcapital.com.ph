How to Refer

Referring is simple and rewarding. Just share the basic details of a business owner you know who may need short-term financing by completing the referral application form. Once your referral applies, our team will review their loan application and keep both you and your referral updated on the status. If the loan is approved and released, you’ll earn 1% of the loan principal as your referral fee (per program terms and conditions).

Ready to start earning?

  1. Complete the Referral Form – Submit the essential details of the business you’re referring.
  2. Stay Informed – We’ll keep you updated by email and phone as the loan moves through approval to release.
  3. Claim your commission– Once the loan is released, your referral reward is credited directly to your bank account — upfront and hassle-free.
