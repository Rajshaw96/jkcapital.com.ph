How to Refer

Referring is simple and rewarding. Just share the basic details of a business owner who need our short-term business loan by completing the referral application form.

Once your referral applies, our team will review his loan application and keep both you and your referral updated on the status. If the loan is approved and released, you’ll earn 1% of the loan principal as your referral fee (see program terms and conditions).

Ready to Start Earning?

  1. Complete the Referral Form. Submit the business details.
  2. Stay informed. We’ll update you by email and phone.
  3. Claim your commission. Rewards are credited directly to your bank account, hassle-free.
